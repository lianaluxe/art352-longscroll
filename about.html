Down the Rabbit Hole draws inspiration from multiple variations of the classic story of Alice in Wonderland, from the Lewis Carroll original, to the Disney movie, to various artistic reinterpretations found online. As a team, we sought to create something that reimagined the classic tale through colorful animation. The illustrations for this project were mainly created by Elise, and the majority of the code was written by Liana, though we each contributed to both aspects of the project.
